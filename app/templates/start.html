Hello, Friend

To play ConnectFour, go over to the Game tab, and start playing!

If you've never played ConnectFour, it's similar to the classic tic-tac-toe game, but the difference is that
in this game, players need to make a row of four (thus connecting four) of their pegs in a straight line, either horizontally, vertically or diagonally.
You will be taking turns with the computer to drop peices into the columns.

Over in the game tab, you can put your peices into the columns by clicking the buttons
on top of each column labelled "Column 1", "Column 2", etc.
You are playing as red, and the computer is yellow.

You can also choose the difficulty at which you play. Here there are levels from 1-3. Please make sure you press
"Submit" if you change from the default, which is 1.
Give yourself a pat on the back if you can beat the computer at all 3 levels.
Warning, computation time at levels 3 is very large, the computer may be slow
